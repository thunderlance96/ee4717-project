Artisanal Bakery honours the art of pastry-making by giving life to their novel creations since its inception.

In the continual process of creativity and innovation, Artisanal Bakery's evolution and growth as an locally recognized bakery brand has been encapsulated throughout generations. From humble beginnings at a small take-away spot in Singapore’s Holland Village, it now boasts 5 stores across Singapore’s sunny island.
