Artisanal Bakery started out with a simple and earnest ambition to share some good old-fashioned pastries. In a world of mass-produced confections, we wanted to go back to basics and bake like our mums and grandmothers used to – the kind of scents, flavours and textures that make us nostalgic for days gone by.

As sticklers for quality and simplicity, we take great pride in what we do and take time to continuously perfect all of our creations – our range of bakery items are hand crafted in small-quantities using fresh produce, wholesome ingredients and no artificial preservatives; and the selection of packaged food and homeware products we carry in our stores represent the work of others in our community of like-minded makers, who share our passion for quality, simplicity and sustainability.

We hope you come by to discover some of the simple goodness found in our stores.
